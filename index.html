why brainstorm?

Getting ONE good idea is hard!

But with enough ideas targeted towards one specific problem, chances are pretty high that you have some really good ideas.

With a structured approach to brainstorming, you might start connecting the dots in new ways.

Maybe in ways that no one did before!
